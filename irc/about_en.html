About Internet Robot Competition 2008


" Internet Robot Competetion 2008 " is an event that planned by TU-Sofia and the Robotic Life Support Lab. of TCU (MI-Tech).
The aim of the competition is to improve the programing skill and to communicate between our universities.
As the first step of this competition, we will make the LEGO robot in this year. In future, other small mobile robots will be admissible.


About LEGO robot


Do you know the " LEGO robot " ?
LEGO robot is constructed with a LEGO MINDSTORMS set. You can control the robot using some programs. It is very easy to make the robot. The programs are very interesting. So, in educational institutions, LEGO MINDSTORMS is used as a teaching material.
